Privacy Policy

At Tech Engine World, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard our users' information.

Information Collection

We collect information when you register on our site, complete forms, or interact with our services. This includes personal data such as your name, email address, and phone number, as well as technical information automatically collected, such as your IP address and browser type.

All information is obtained with your consent and is used to enhance the user experience, personalize content, and optimize the performance of our services.

Use and Protection of Data

The information collected is used to process transactions, manage accounts, and send you updates and important notifications. At Tech Engine World, we adopt state-of-the-art security measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration of your data.

All data is stored on secure servers, and regular backups are performed to ensure service continuity and reliability. Only authorized personnel have access to personal information, and all our employees are bound by strict confidentiality obligations.

Your Rights and Policy Changes

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. If you wish to exercise these rights or have questions about our privacy policy, you can contact us at any time through our contact form.

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be published on our website and, if necessary, notified to our users. We recommend reviewing this section periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
